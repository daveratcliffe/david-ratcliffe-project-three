Choosies

Choosies is a game with origins that date back to ancient Greece. The rules are simple. There are two players. One player is even, and the other is odd. Each player chooses to raise any number of fingers on one hand. They then display their chosen number to each other. Both players' fingers are then added together. If the sum of all fingers are even, you win. If it's odd, your opponent wins.
